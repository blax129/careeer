Work with us

Work hard. Be proud. Have fun.

We are FIFA. We're headquartered in Zurich, but we belong to the world, with offices on every continent. We are more than 850 hard-working, passionate people from more than 60 countries, and more than 40% of us are women. We are proud to belong to this global organisation, and we like to work hard (and have fun).

A presence around the world

We are headquartered at the Home of FIFA in Zurich. We also have offices in Paris, Miami and Rabat and a further 11 Regional Offices around the world. From these regional offices in Barbados, India, Malaysia, New Zealand, Panama, Paraguay, Senegal, Congo, South Africa and the United Arab Emirates, we aim to provide closer support and guidance to our member associations and drive global football development all over the world.

Zurich – the best of both worlds

If you're looking to live in one of the cleanest, safest cities in the world, where on a clear day you can see the snow on the distant peaks of the Alps, then you've come to the right place. But if you're looking to experience the bright lights and daily distractions of a bustling cosmopolitan city... then you too have come to the right place! Whether you're a family, a young couple, or living solo, Zurich and its surrounding areas are a truly special place to call home.

Want to explore? Zurich is a superb base from which to travel across Europe and beyond. If it's the great outdoors that you're after, then outside the city you're surrounded by endless hiking and walking routes all through the year, with the lure of the Alps only an hour away by car or train. We're sure you'll love it as much as we do.

The little extras

The biggest benefit of a career at FIFA is the privilege to work for the beautiful game and create something that matters. Alongside that privilege, we also offer some perks & benefits:

Vacation

Maintain a healthy work/life balance with a generous vacation allowance.

Continuous learning

We offer a variety of training and education to help you achieve your development goals.

Volunteering

Make an impact beyond your regular role and bond with colleagues by volunteering with one of our partners.

Health and fitness

We support our staff's health and fitness with either an onsite gym or a gym allowance, depending on the location.

Performance bonus

In addition to your base salary you are entitled to receive a performance-based bonus.

Pension

We support our employees' future with competitive pension benefits.

Home office

Up to two days of home office per week, contingent on job requirements and line manager approval.

Sustainability

We support employees using public transport.
